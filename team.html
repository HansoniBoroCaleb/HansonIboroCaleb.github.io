Mr Adeyemi Oshin

ACIPM MD/CEO

He holds over 28 years of experience in Human Resources Management, Property & Estate Management, Outsourcing and Logistics, Vendor Management & Payments. He started his Human Resources career with Flour Mills of Nigeria Plc. between in 1993 to 1999. He had a brief stint with Concord Press Nigeria Ltd as the National Sales/Circulation Manager before joining Intercontinental Bank Plc. (now Access Bank) where he was between 2000 -2013. He was the Pioneer Managing Director of Integrity Press Limited, a subsidiary of Intercontinental Bank. Yemi is a professional member of Chartered Institute of Personnel Management (CIPM); Association of Outsourcing Practioners of Nigeria (AOPN); chartered institute of Management (NIM); Academy for Entrepreneurial Studies (AES). Yemi holds a B.Sc. degree in Psychology from the University of Ibadan, Diploma in Business & Industrial Law from the University of Lagos and MBA in Human Resources Management from Lagos State University. He has also attended many Professional and leadership training programmes facilitated by such institutions as the Lagos Business School, London Management Centre (LMC), London and other Chartered profession body both Local and International.
