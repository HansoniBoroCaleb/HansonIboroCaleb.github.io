About H&B Energy and Logistic Services Limited

H&B Energy and Logistic Services Limited was established in 2013 with the goal of becoming one of the best People and Business Management Consulting firms in Nigeria. With offices in Lagos and twelve other locations, we pride ourselves as specialists in our chosen field to be committed to the ideals of tapping new sources of value, which also means sharing ownership of outstanding results. We are driven by a sense of mutually beneficial relationships with our clients, with a view to creating high performance teams, and supporting business operations that conforms with industry best practice. As one of the fastest growing People Management and HR consultancy firm in Nigeria today, We have the capacity to ensure returns on investment in the following areas: Personnel Outsourcing, Human Resources Consulting, Training and Manpower Development, Third Party Sales Support, Logistics Services and Real Estate Management. At the heart of our business is integrity and the unique implementation structure that ensures that all services and business transaction are delivered at the highest standards. Leveraging several years of experience, expertise, proven methodologies, with a crop of qualified Management team, H & B is confident that we have the capabilities to deliver quality service and business partnership from start to end.
